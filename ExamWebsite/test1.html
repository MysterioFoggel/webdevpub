    Randen : Object Number - 1

    class - Gallus gallus domesticus :- Ggd


    Special Containment Procedures: Randen poses no real threat to any living being bigger than it. However it is worth to note that Randen has been known to attack anything smaller than itself. These include most birds, other Ggd, and in rare cases Fc.

    Randen is, as with all other objects of type: "Ggd" to be kept in a secure area atleast 1*1*3 m in size, with a standard model door supporting a locking mekanism. This area includes a constant food and water supply, This area should never be without any of these supplies for more than 24 hours.


    Description: Randen is a small bipedal animal approximately 70 cm tall. Randen is coated in a thick layer of feathers providing protection against weather and minor injuries. Randen is a omnivore and eats most substances that one would call food. However Randen has in several cases tried to eat things other than food. These include: insulation, feathers, and styrofoam. This behavior could harm Randen and should be stopped imediently.
